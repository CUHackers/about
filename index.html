CUhackit

January 25 & 26, 2020

CUhackit is the premier hackathon of South Carolina. We are a multidisciplinary team of dedicated and passionate individuals who are passionate about hackathons. We endeavor to provide the ultimate hackathon experience to college students from all backgrounds.

Join us for CUhackit as we embrace the third year of our groundbreaking event. We will have sessions lead by experts in their areas, the full Watt Family Innovation Center facilities and capabilities, as well as ample caffiene.

CUhackit also boasts an onsite career fair attended by industry leaders from South Carolina and its surrounding areas. They cannot wait to meet you and are great resources for internships and full-time work.

We cannot wait to see you here!
